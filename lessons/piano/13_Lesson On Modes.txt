_Modes are variations of the **major and minor scales**, each starting from a different note within the scale.  
They provide unique tonal colors and are the foundation for understanding jazz, rock, and modal harmony._
---

### 🧠 Diatonic Chord Guide
**Major Scale:**  
I, ii, iii, IV, V, vi, vii°  
I∆, iim7, iiim7, IV∆, V7, vim7, viiø  

**Natural Minor:**  
i, ii°, bIII, iv, v, bVI, bVII  
im7, iiø, bIII∆, ivm7, vm7, bVI∆, bVII7  

**Harmonic Minor:**  
i, ii°, bIII+, iv, V, bVI, vii°  
im∆, iiø, bIII∆#5, ivm7, V7, bVI∆, vii°7  

**Melodic Minor:**  
i, iim, bIII+, IV, V, vi°, vii°  
im∆, iim7, bIII∆#5, IV7, V7, viø, viiø
